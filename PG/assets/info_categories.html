DIYgenomics

is a personal genome reference app for your data in a variety of conditions such as health risk, drug response, and athletic performance.

23andMe private data upload

To upload your 23andMe data, email your zip file as an email attachment, or download the zip file directly from the 23andMe website on the mobile. In the downloads manager, click to open the file, and an option to import it into DIYgenomics should appear.

To delete your data from the device, select 'Delete Personal Data" form the menu.
